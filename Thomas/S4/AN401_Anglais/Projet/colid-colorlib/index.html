Frequently Asked Questions

By going through the story behind this product, by the delivery locations, or by customizing the PlaceMaker, we answer all your questions !

Why create this product ?

As students, we noticed that the place in our kitchens is often non existent when renting a small apartment.
We also know that closet space is a problem for every modest home.
Whether for a large family, a survivalist or for anyone else who feels concerned, the place in the cupboards is a problem and we thought of a solution.

We propose you an investment, certainly consequent, but it will avoid you to spend it in a kitchen that you will surely change more than once. Our product will be able to follow you no matter the changes in your lives, in your kitchens or in your apartments

PlaceMaker is the result of this reflection.
